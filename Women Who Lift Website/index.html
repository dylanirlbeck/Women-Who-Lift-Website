Women Who Lift

Mission

Women Who Lift is a student organization striving to empower women through weightlifting and health-centered workshops.

About

We aim to create a supportive community for women of all lifting backgrounds through weekly meetings about lifting technique, exercise physiology, and nutrition, group gym visits lead by more experienced lifters, and guest speaker events. In addition to providing our members with accurate information about the mechanics of lifting, WWL strives to foster a dialogue about womanhood - from a physiological and sociological perspective.
